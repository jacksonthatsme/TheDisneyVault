Discover a Disney Treasure

Bring a little magic into your home. The Disney Vault has been re-opened and we are bringing a handful of Disney classics back into the light. These movies are the finest that Disney has to offer, including Blu-ray, DVD and a Digital Copy's of these beloved films. Beautifully restored and digitally remastered, The Disney Vault classics — Series 1, will reawaken fond memories of Disney experiences, as well as bring the Disney magic that lies in these films to a brand new audience.

How it Works

Look for the Disney Vault display at your local movie store to bring home a piece of Disney history today!

Order online and we will send you a mystery.

All Disney Vault movies are sealed in their own vault box. Only when you open the vault can you discover what Disney classic lies inside. Collect all of the videos in the series!

About

The Disney Vault is a project by graphic designer Jackson Lynch. While the concept of the Disney Vault has been used casually over the years by Disney, Jackson sought to rebrand the moratorium experience, adding the flair of mystery that The Disney Vault suggests. If you would like to see more of Jackson Lynch's work, please visit his portfolio website.

Colophon

This website was designed and hand coded by graphic designer Jackson Lynch. The typefaces chosen are Crimson Text and Silentina by Ray Larabie. This website is using jQuery seeThru to make the dust effect, SimpleModal for the dialogue boxes, and has reworked Chris Wilson's Pitch Detector, to make the magic happen. The tools used were Coda 2, Emmet, Compass, and LiveReload.
